Welcome to My Website - where I get to tell you about my Favorite Movies of All Time

The American President

This movie is a romantic comedy-drama that tells the story of a widowed U.S. President who falls in love with an environmental lobbyist.
It explores themes of love, politics, and the challenges of balancing personal and professional life.

Meet Joe Black

This movie is a fantasy romance that tells the story of a wealthy media mogul who makes a deal with Death,
who takes on the form of a young man to experience life as a human.
It explores themes of love, mortality, and the meaning of life.

The Adjustment Bureau

This movie is a science fiction romance that tells the story of a young candidate running for the U.S. Senate who falls in love with a ballerina.
It explores themes of fate, free will, and the power of love to overcome obstacles.
It is based on a short story by Philip K. Dick titled "Adjustment Team."
